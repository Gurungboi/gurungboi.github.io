Privacy Policy

Citizenmate

Effective Date: March 26, 2026

Citizenmate ("the App") is an educational application designed to help users prepare for citizenship tests. The App is developed by Sunil Gurung and is intended solely for educational purposes.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from its users. No accounts are required to use the App, and no personal details such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data are gathered at any point.

Data Storage

Since no user data is collected, no data is stored on external servers or databases. Any progress or preferences within the App are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to the developer or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, the App is safe for users of all ages.
